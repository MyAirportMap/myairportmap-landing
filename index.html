Install on iPhone

  1. Open myairportmap.com/open in Safari
  2. Tap Share (square with arrow)
  3. Tap Add to Home Screen
  4. Launch from your new icon

Install on Android

  1. Open myairportmap.com/open in Chrome
  2. Tap the ⋮ menu
  3. Tap Install app or Add to Home screen
  4. Launch from your new icon
